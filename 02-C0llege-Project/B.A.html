PROFILE

The college offers three year Bachelor of Arts (B.A) degree programme as approved by the Andhra University. The eligibility for admission in to First Year B. A programme is a pass in Andhra Pradesh Intermediate Board Exam or its equivalent. Students from all branches of study at Intermediate are eligible for admission into B.A programme. Every year 160 students are admitted in to first year B.A The examination system is semester based and internal assessment is done by Mid exams. Student’s performance in exams is evaluated and results are awarded in Grade system. The programme is offered in three combinations. History / Economics / Politics History / Geography / Travel &Tourism Special Telugu / Politics / Public administration

Objectives

• Students are asked to explore the local surroundings and observe the activities of social communities engaged in different skills and materials.
• The students are motivated to make models of historical monuments, charts indicating the effects of volcanoes or earthquakes, crossword games or puzzles
• to encourage them to explore higher levels of knowledge in different disciplines.
• to promote problem-solving abilities and creative thinking in the citizens of tomorrow.
• to introduce students to different ways of collecting and processing data and information in specific disciplines, and help them arrive at conclusions, and to generate new insights and knowledge in the process.
• To produce students with a commitment and ethical value.
• To enhance the productive employment of the students in the field of global and social activities with a technological view and value added inputs.
