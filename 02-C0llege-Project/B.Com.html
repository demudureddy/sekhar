PROFILE

The college offers Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) degree programme as approved by the Andhra University. The eligibility for admission into first year B.Com programme is a pass in Intermediate Exam of the Board of Intermediate, AP or its equivalent. Students from all branches of study at Intermediate are eligible. Every year 250 students are admitted in to first year B.Com. This department has been in existence since the inception of this college in the year 1973. During the long journey the department turned out a large number of students who are well settled, professionally. The strength of the department is a pool of experienced teachers who have been teaching in the department since its inception in 1973, and even today, are actively involved in teaching and guiding the department. The examination system is annual based and partly internal assessment for some subjects. Student’s performance in exams is evaluated and results are awarded in Grade system. For the award of internal assessment marks the department insists on student’s regularity to classes and also the performance at internal exams conducted periodically.

PEDAGOGY

Subject Curriculum And Teaching Aids:

Curricular subjects are dealt with for four sessions each per week during a semester. Besides conventional blackboard and regular classroom environment, teaching is carried out with the use of LCD projectors, audio visual equipment in seminar halls designated for the course. A well-equipped computer lab with internet facility is available for the students to make use of information technology for learning besides acquainting with on-line examination system on subject areas to fine tune the fundamentals necessary for their placements. However, the subjects and subject delivery mechanism is periodically revised to suit the market. The department also carries out mentor – mentee activities to enable slow learners to meet the mentor every week to obtain counseling on curricular and personal improvement aspects.

Value Add-On Programmes In B.Com And BBA:

Two add-on courses are given to the students one each in 1st and 2nd year. Four sessions in a week are demarcated to deal with the add-on program. The aim of the department is to broaden the academic scope by including specific subjects as add-on programs to fill in the gap between the acquired and the required market related knowledge.
The Value Addition Teachers in the department have developed Add-on programmes that add extra value to our students for success in social life as well as at places of work. The value addition programmes are offered to all students in the department and it is for them to choose. The add-on programmes offered are –

Add-On Programes

• Six Sigma
• Value Proposition in Business Models
• Dealing with difficult people
• Emotional Intelligence
• Ethics in Corporate houses
• Financial Derivatives etc.
• Tally – Computerised Accounting Package

Business Line and Harvard Business Review Programmes in B.Com and BBA:

Employment is not the only objective of education, building life skills and personality development are also important, keeping this in mind department provided six session per week for the Business Line classes to provide business news to the students and monthly four sessions for Harvard Business Review (HBR) to the students, and during this session students will present the articles from the HBR under the guidance of the dean and the faculty, which helps the students to enrich their knowledge and presentation skills and makes them to stand in Global Competition.

Soft Skill Development for B.Com and BBA Students

Four sessions per week are set apart to provide the students with Skill Development where classes are held to hone the soft skills, better communication, to improve team work and Kinesics. Here the student gets an opportunity to learn about oneself, apply that knowledge, participate in leadership tasks, Group discussions etc that will transform them into thorough management professionals

OBJECTIVES AND GOALS OF B.COM PROGRAMME

• To provide a sound foundation in Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting, Management Accounting, Taxation and Statistics.
• To integrate knowledge skills and attitude to surge the employability of students.
• To benefit industry by providing it suitably trained students in the area of commerce.

FACULTY

The department is run by experienced faculty with an understanding of student needs to the core. Each faculty is assigned with a group of students. Faculty pays personal attention to each one of the assigned students to make them successful in an increasingly competitive world. The faculty also takes active interest in research and publications in leading journals, participate and presenting research papers in national seminars to keep themselves abreast with the management theory and practice

SPECIAL FEATURES OF THE PROGRAM

Advanced Learners:

We have opened a special window for students who have the ability as well as interest to learn and grow. We call them as advanced learners. Based on student’s interest, we have a separate batch of advanced learners and teach them extra value additions, over and above the University prescribed syllabus.

TRAINING FOR CPT EXAM:

We are working on a plan to offer training to our students for CPT, an entrance exam conducted by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. Students admitted in June 2015 for the academic year 2015-16 and opt for CPT will be given intensive training. The purpose of this programme is to Support students who desire to excel in the field of accountancy.
