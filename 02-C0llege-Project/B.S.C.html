PROFILE

The college offers three year Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) degree programme as approved by the Andhra University. The eligibility for admission in to B. Sc programme is pass in Intermediate Board Exam or its equivalent conducted by govt. of Andhra Pradesh Admission norms are prescribed by the Government of Andhra Pradesh and the Andhra University. The programme is offered in a wide range of options and combinations in Physical Sciences and Life Sciences. The total intake is 1452 students each year. The science wing of UG has library, well equipped laboratory , Computers, Digital Trainer Kits, Microscopes etc for physical sciences and Life sciences as well. All the faculty are experienced in handling theory and practical sessions. The faculty strength is 50.
