PROFILE


Undergraduate programe in Dr. L Bullayya college has its existence since 1973. B-com was the first course to be offered .The college was sanctioned govt. aid in the year 1976. At present the college offers UG courses in various disciplines. Principal is the head of the Institution and Dean holds the responsibility of all academic activities supported by two Asst. Deans. Present student strength in UG is 2653 . Faculty strength is 65. The college is supported by 10 administrative staff and 6 technical staff assisting in laboratories. The courses are run in block-1, Block-2 and Block-4. All courses are approved by and affiliated to Andhra University and are of three years duration run in semester pattern. Syllabus is prescribed by the University. The conduct of semester-end theory and practical examinations and issue of degrees are the prerogative of Andhra University. The college conducts periodical tests for internal assessment. The college environment facilitates for student’s overall development and growth by encouraging them not only in academics but also in participation of events like cultural, literary, sports, NCC, NSS. The college has good infrastructure, well equipped Laboratories, Library, Seminar Halls, Playfields. Sports students are given due encouragement in coaching and for participation in state and National level competitions. Ever since the inception of the college many students won championship trophies, Medals and shields and brought laurels to the college . NCC cadets also make their mark in all events. NSS volunteers are actively engaged in community and social work. The undergraduate programme has two divisions for academic related functions.


Commerce : B-Com and BBM

Arts & Sciences : B.Sc and B.A.

INFRASTRUCTURE


Library

The Central Library is located in the second floor of Administrative Block ,Block-4 with a plinth area of 4500 Sft. catering to the needs of about 2643 user population which includes students, teaching and non-teaching staff.

The library also caters to the needs of students preparing for competitive examinations like TOEFL, GRE, GMAT, IELTS, CAT etc., and entrance exams like C.A. and I.C.W.A. It has a spacious well-furnished and equipped reading room with a seating capacity of 150 at a time. The library has also adopted Open Access System and classified arrangement of books, which increases the utility of available resources to an optimum level.

The library has well qualified librarian and adequate supporting staff. Almost all the activities related to management of a library like cataloguing, circulation, and Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) have been automated with the help of SOUL (Software for University Libraries) version.2003 developed by INFLIBNET.

Classroom : Students are provided with spacious classrooms with Furniture and fixtures.

Seminar Hall: The UG college has a well-equipped air conditioned seminar hall with an LCD projector.

Principal-UG

Dr .Y .Poli Reddy made a start-up of his career as Physical Director by joining Dr .Lankapalli Bullayya College in May 1994. He has Post Graduated from Acharya Nagarjuna University and got awarded his Doctorate from the same University in the year 2012. He got to his credit the qualifications to match his profession from the arenas of B.P.Ed., M.P.Ed., NIS, Post Graduate Diploma in Yoga. He was a Distinction achiever and a Gold Medalist in the spheres of Education and academics.

His journey in the college has begun from 1994 as Physical Director and continues till date in the capacity of Principal (FAC). During the tenure he also served as Sports Inspector for Visakhapatnam Port Trust on Leave and Lien basis for a period of two years. After being served as Physical Director for a span of thirteen years in Intermediate College, he was promoted as Lecturer in Physical Education for Degree College and then became Principal (FAC) of the College from July 2016.

He developed as a strong academic pioneer in the field of sports by his participation in various International Workshops and Conferences held at Goa, Guntur and other parts of India. He got a publication to his credit in the Journal of National Associates of Physical Education and Sports, with reference of ISSN 2229-7049 on the topic “Effect of Low, Medium and High Intensity Polymeric Training on Selected Psychological Equipment”.

Being a sportsmen by himself, he bagged Gold Medal in the National Badminton Doubles Championship and another Gold Medal in Andhra Pradesh Teachers Tournament, Badminton, Singles; he was also a National Medal Winner in Table Tennis. Besides his person sporting achievements, he also served as Selection Committee Member at District, State and National levels for Cricket and Badminton events, also served as District Secretary for School Games Federation of India.

He has made Dr.Lankapalli Bullayya College as a Landmark for the sport aspirants in the coastal Andhra through his innovative leadership and guidance in the sporting events of all spheres. The college has gained the laurels from the Inter-University, District, National and International sporting championships through his relentless efforts. As a Team Manager of the college, he motivated the sporting personalities to mould them as professionals in their events and he is an indirect force to get the medals from all events at different levels.
